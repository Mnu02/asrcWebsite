About this Project

This website is meant to expose the injustices that have been committed against Native Americans and how those injustices are presently ongoing. The user is supposed to think critically to question the information they will come across here. Nothing is taken for granted. Analyse everything and don't leave anything to chance. You might realize that you are participating in the dispossession of Native Americans.
